# Internship Program

## Internship Overview
Positiveway Solutions runs a hands-on internship program for students
interested in Data Science, AI, and Web Development. Interns work on real
client-facing projects, not just training exercises, with mentorship from
the core team throughout.

## Application Process
To apply, share your resume and a short note about which track interests
you (Data Science / AI, Web Development, or Design) through this
WhatsApp assistant or the contact channel shared by our team. Shortlisted
candidates are contacted for a brief conversation before onboarding.

## Resume Submission
You can submit your resume by sending it as a message here, or by asking
this assistant for the current submission email/link, which will be kept
up to date by the admin team.

## Internship FAQs
- Internships are typically project-based with a fixed short deadline.
- Interns get real deliverables to add to their portfolio.
- Remote participation is generally supported.
- Certificates are issued on successful completion of the assigned project.
